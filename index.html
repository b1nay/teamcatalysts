About

The Catalysts - also referred to as Team Catalysts is an active group of hackers / developers / entrepreneurs who have come together conquer hackfests, hackathons, competitions or any form of digital combat. The team solely comprises of B.Sc. CSIT aspirants of Tribhuvan University - Mechi Multiple Campus.

From hardware to software, the Team shows excellent craftmanship - building beautifully innovative solutions(s).
