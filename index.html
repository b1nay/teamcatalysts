About

The Catalysts - also referred to as Team Catalysts - is an active group of hackers/developers/entrepreneurs who have come together conquer hackfests, hackathons, competitions or any form of digital combat. The team solely comprises of B.Sc. CSIT aspirants of Tribhuvan University IOST at Mechi Multiple Campus.
